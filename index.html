Privacy Policy for Namma Workers

Effective Date:20.04.2026

Namma Workers ("we", "our", "us") operates the Namma Workers mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and profile details
  • Location Data: For workers, the app collects location while they have enabled Online mode. This includes background location so nearby customers can discover available workers and send job requests even when the app is minimized. Location sharing stops when the worker switches Offline.
  • Usage Data: App interactions, device type, and performance data
  • Authentication Data: Authentication information provided through Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To connect users with service professionals
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To communicate important updates or notifications
  • To share a worker's live location with nearby customers only while the worker is Online.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your data only in the following cases:

  • With service professionals to fulfill user requests
  • With trusted third-party services (e.g., Firebase) for authentication and analytics
  • When required by law or legal processes

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

6. User Rights

  • You can access or update your personal data
  • You can request deletion of your account and data
  • You can withdraw permissions like location access anytime from device settings

7. Children's Privacy

Namma Workers is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services such as:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Analytics)
  • Google Play Services
  • Authentication Data: Authentication information provided through Google Sign-In and Firebase Authentication.

These services may collect information as governed by their own privacy policies.

9. Permissions Used

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Location (Foreground and Background) – Used only for workers who enable Online mode. This allows customers to discover nearby available workers and receive job requests even when the app is minimized. Location sharing stops when the worker switches Offline.
  • Internet – for app functionality

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

11. Background Location Usage

Background location is used only for workers who voluntarily enable Online mode. It allows the app to continue sharing the worker's live location with nearby customers while the app is minimized so that job requests can be received. When the worker switches Offline, background location collection stops immediately.
